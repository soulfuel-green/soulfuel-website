Step-by-Step Process

Our comprehensive 8-step process ensures consistent quality and sustainable production.

1

Raw Material Collection

Collection of agricultural waste from farms across India, including rice husks, wheat straw, and corn stalks.

2

Quality Assessment

Thorough inspection for moisture content, contamination, and suitability for biomass conversion.

3

Pre-processing

Cleaning, crushing, and sizing of raw materials to prepare them for the conversion process.

4

Drying & Conditioning

Controlled drying to achieve optimal moisture levels for efficient biomass fuel production.

5

Pelletizing/Briquetting

Compression of processed materials into uniform pellets or briquettes using advanced machinery.

6

Quality Control

Rigorous testing for calorific value, ash content, and durability to ensure premium quality.

7

Packaging & Storage

Professional packaging in moisture-resistant bags and storage in controlled environments.

8

Distribution

Efficient delivery network ensuring timely supply to customers across India.
